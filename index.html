I’m Joe, a commercial CGI artist and digital creative based in High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire.

For more than four years I’ve worked across broadcast animation, product visualisation, digital marketing and interactive web development. My work spans the full creative pipeline: modelling, texturing, lighting and rendering through to compositing, branded content and frontend implementation.

I bring visual craft together with production awareness, collaborative problem-solving and a focus on building workflows that make ambitious work achievable.
